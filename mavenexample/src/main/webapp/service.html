About Krubka

Your reliable property consultant

Krubka.com is established in 2018 and has headquarter in Bangkok. Service was developed by group of realty experts whose goal is to make it easier for foreigners to own properties in Thailand

We used to be expats once and know how difficult it is to own properties abroad.  There are many things to do when you are in a new country. You have to learn new language, culture and people. And, on top of the list you need accommodation. We have been through this hassle. The problem is the same regardless of where you are whether you are in Europe, Asia or US. You cannot find a trust partner to begin with. Language barrier makes the problem even harder. Our goal is to help you overcome this challenge. We aim to make the process easier for you to find property you need and for developers to find right buyers. Since we don't do this for a living, our service is free of charge. 

Find your dream property We select properties only from renowned developers. You can be confident of its products and services. If you cannot find what you're looking for, please directly contact us by clicking the blue button on the top right.
